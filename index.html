Our Journey

1977
The Beginning: Delhi Queen was founded in Tilak Nagar with a vision to provide quality men's wear at honest prices. Started with traditional ethnic wear.
1990s
Expansion: Expanded into formal wear and established our own manufacturing unit. Became a trusted name for wedding sherwanis in West Delhi.
2000s
Wholesale Growth: Started wholesale operations, partnering with retailers across Delhi NCR. Introduced casual wear collection.
2024
Digital Transformation: Launched our online presence, bringing 47 years of trust to the digital world. Now serving customers across India!
